Our Comprehensive Home Inspection Services

Ensuring the safety and integrity of your property with detailed, quality-focused inspections.

Standard TREC Inspection Includes:

  • Foundations
  • Grading and drainage
  • Roof coverings
  • Walls (Interior / Exterior)
  • Ceilings and Floors
  • Windows
  • Stairs (Exterior steps / Interior stairs)
  • Fireplace and Chimney
  • Porches, balconies, decks, carports
  • Driveways and Sidewalks
  • Electrical service entrance and Panels
  • Branch circuits
  • Heating equipment
  • Cooling equipment
  • Ducts, chases and vents
  • Water supply and fixtures
  • Drains, wastes and vents
  • Water heating equipment
  • Hydro massage equipment
  • Dishwasher
  • Disposal
  • Range exhaust vent
  • Microwave
  • Trash compactor
  • Mechanical exhaust fans and heaters
  • Garage door operators
  • Doorbell
  • Dryer vent

A standard TREC (Texas Real Estate Commission) inspection provides a thorough assessment of these critical home components.

Enhance Your Inspection with Additional Services:

Infrared Camera Inspections Additional Fee

Our non-invasive infrared camera inspections identify hidden issues such as heat loss, moisture intrusion, and electrical problems. This helps maintain energy efficiency, enhances safety, and detects problems early, saving you from costly repairs.

Irrigation System Inspection Additional Fee

A comprehensive inspection of your irrigation system identifies leaks, ensuring effective water use and reducing waste. Regular checks extend system lifespan, saving money on repairs and promoting healthier, lush landscapes.
