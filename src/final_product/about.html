Overall Inspection Services: Quality Over Quantity

At Overall Inspection Services, LLC, our philosophy is simple: we believe a truly thorough home inspection takes time. That's why we dedicate our focus to inspecting only ONE home per day. This allows us to meticulously assess every detail, identify potential issues, and provide you with a comprehensive understanding of your property's condition.

Our commitment to quality ensures that both home sellers and homebuyers can make informed decisions, avoiding costly surprises and gaining peace of mind.

Meet Your Inspector: Ken Cofer

Ken Cofer is a highly experienced Professional Home Inspector with TREC License #20262. With over 30 years of experience in both residential and commercial construction industries, Ken brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every inspection.

His approach goes beyond simply delivering a report. Ken walks clients around the property after the physical inspection, explaining the condition of the dwelling and answering all questions to ensure the report makes complete sense. This personalized attention ensures you're fully informed and confident in your property decisions.
