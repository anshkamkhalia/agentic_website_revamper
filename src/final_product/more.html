Frequently Asked Questions

What does a typical home inspection cover?

Our standard TREC inspection is comprehensive, covering major systems and components like foundations, roofing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and more. For a full list, please visit our Services page.

How long does an inspection take?

Typically, an inspection takes approximately 1.5 to 2 hours per 1000 total square feet, ensuring thoroughness without rushing.

Can I be present during the inspection?

Absolutely! We encourage clients to be present. Ken Cofer will walk you through the property post-inspection, explaining findings and answering any questions to ensure clarity.

What is the InterNACHI Buy-Back Guarantee?

As an InterNACHI certified inspector, Ken Cofer offers a Buy-Back Guarantee. If we miss anything covered in our standards of practice, InterNACHI will buy your home back. Terms and conditions apply; please visit the InterNACHI website for details.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes, we proudly offer a 10% discount to retired, separated, and/or active military personnel as a thank you for your service.
